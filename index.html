DM Trigger Guide

Use these signals to decide WHEN to DM. Never DM cold — wait for 1+ strong signal.

Like x2+ quickly
Likely profile visit. Action: Wait 10-60 mins, then send Warm Opener.
Follow after engagement
High intent. Action: Send Warm Opener within 2 hours.
Replies with pain
Hot lead. Action: DM Deep-Pain question immediately.
Bookmarks / Saves
Quiet interest — watch for another signal; follow up with value after 1 day.
Multiple engagements over 48h
Very warm. Action: DM qualification + permission offer.

Timing Rules

  • Warm Opener: within 2 hours of trigger.
  • Qualification: after they reply to the opener same day.
  • Permission Offer: only after pain is clear.
  • Ghost follow-up: 24–48 hours after no reply.

Quick Tips

  • Prefer short DMs. Ask one question. Diagnose.
  • Log every DM in your CRM sheet after sending.
  • Use the first comment to place less-important hashtags or links.
  • Respect "no" — don't chase buyers. Nurture instead.
